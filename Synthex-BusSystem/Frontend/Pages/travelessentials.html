Luggage Allowance Reminder

Standard tickets include one large bag (up to 20kg) plus one piece of hand luggage. Additional bags can be added during booking for a fee. Oversized or additional luggage not pre-booked may incur charges at the station.

Frequently Asked Questions

We recommend arriving at the station at least 30 minutes before your scheduled departure time. For busy routes or during peak travel periods (holidays, weekends), arriving 45-60 minutes early is advisable. This allows time for boarding, luggage storage, and any unexpected delays. Boarding typically closes 10 minutes before departure.

If you miss your bus, what happens next depends on your ticket type:
  • Flexible tickets: You can board the next available service on the same day at no extra cost, subject to seat availability.
  • Standard tickets: You can change to another service for a small administration fee, subject to availability.
  • Restricted tickets: These cannot be changed, and you would need to purchase a new ticket.
If you know in advance that you'll miss your bus, contact our customer service as soon as possible.

Yes, passengers are welcome to bring food and non-alcoholic drinks on board. We recommend bringing snacks with minimal odor and that are not messy to eat. Hot food and alcoholic beverages are not permitted. All GoTimely coaches have waste bins, and we ask that you dispose of your rubbish properly. On longer journeys, coaches will make scheduled stops where you can purchase food and beverages.

Most GoTimely coaches offer free WiFi and USB charging ports. The WiFi is suitable for browsing and messaging, though streaming services may be limited due to bandwidth constraints, especially on busy routes. Power outlets are available on premium coaches. Please note that on older vehicles or during technical issues, these services might be temporarily unavailable. We recommend bringing a portable power bank as a backup for longer journeys.

GoTimely is committed to making travel accessible for all passengers:
  • Most of our coaches are equipped with wheelchair lifts and designated wheelchair spaces.
  • Assistance dogs are welcome on all services.
  • Priority seating is available for passengers with reduced mobility.
  • Staff are trained to assist passengers with various accessibility needs.
To ensure we can best accommodate your needs, please contact our Accessible Travel team at least 48 hours before your journey on 0800 234 6789.
